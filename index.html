''In a world where most women rely too much on the opinions of others, we believe they have to focus more on themselves, their own opinion and succes, in order to achieve personal growth and acceptance.''

Women's gets affected by advertisments, influencers and other posts on social media. Many women become insecure, unhappy, and will start focusing too much on others instead of themselves. What happens when you are confident? You will love and accept yourself, just the way you are.

You will no longer care about what others think of you or what you see on social media, because you focus on yourself and your own journey to your succes. With this vision in mind, we created BLISS. A brand that motivates women all over the world to become the very best version of themselves. Growth, Positivity, Confidence and Acceptance is what we stand for. With our retreats, we will give you the tools to run your own life, instead of letting others do that for you. We will help you to find your BLISS.



What we have to offer...

Besides our social media pages such as the Instagram page and the Facebook community group that you can join, we also offer retreats. Our goal is to let you regain your personal growth, positivity, confidence and acceptance in order to achieve your bliss. Therefore, we give you and many other young women the opportunity to experience retreats to find your true inner self. A week or weekend full of fun activities, workshops and experts in the most beautiful region of Bali. You will come back stronger, empowered and confident. Does this already sound like music to your ears?
